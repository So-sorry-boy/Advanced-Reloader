Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 27, 2025

Advanced Reloader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users. The extension runs entirely locally in your browser to reload the current tab based on your settings.

No data is sent to any servers or third parties. Your browsing activity and tab information remain private and are not tracked.
